    NIHILISM

    Nihilism challenges the essence of existence and meaning. It's not just emptiness; it's a profound abyss negating all purpose. Amid confusion and dread, it prompts reflection. Yet, it's liberating, freeing from absolutes. Facing it, we must guard against despair, seeking understanding. Does nihilism truly exist? Perhaps it's an extreme concept, hiding unseen order. Regardless, confronting it reveals personal truths. In this uncertain world, nihilism may be our ultimate reality, but it's through facing it that we find meaning.

    PHILOSOPHY

    On Earth, the small to medium-sized gallery and exhibition industry has matured considerably, yet it is difficult to find spaces of similar high standards on Mars, which the reason Obscure Gallery been created. Although Obscure Gallery, born in 2020, is built on a passion for art and respect for creators. We are committed to providing a platform for artists to showcase their work, while allowing viewers to interact deeply with the art.
